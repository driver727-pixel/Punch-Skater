# Archetypes

The live forge uses ten courier archetypes. In practice they function like schools, factions,
or professional lineages: each one carries a worldview, a stat identity, and a role in the City's conflict web.

Hidden truths should be tagged explicitly so the archetype file can function as writer-facing canon
without leaking future beats into public copy.

---

## The Knights Technarchy
> *"The Dark Lights see everything. Serve or be disappeared."*

**Public:** Cyber ninja zealots serving the secretive Dark Lights. They move sacred packages between
hidden temples and build their reputation through fear and precision.

**Internal:** Their pressure enters through the hidden artifact spine rather than through public faction knowledge.

**Strengths:** Maximum Stealth, elite Speed. Excels in surveillance-heavy districts. Penalty: low Rep.

---

## Qu111s
> *"The truth is in the data. We will release it."*

**Public:** A guerrilla journalist network protecting volatile proof-carriers while preparing a truth
dump big enough to destabilize the UCA.

**Reveal:** Their release only matters if the surrounding chaos is big enough to make the city
listen.

**Strengths:** Maximum Rep, high Grit. Thrives in Nightshade and open districts. Penalty: low Stealth.

---

## Iron Curtains
> *"Overthrow the oligarchy. By any means necessary."*

**Public:** A revolutionary front trafficking in weapons and leverage.

**Internal:** Their myth, rhetoric, and recruitment pool matter more in public than their actual
command structure.

**Reveal:** Hidden beneath the public insurgent image is a UCA false-flag design.

**Strengths:** High Grit, balanced stats. Adaptable across all districts. Steady performers over specialists.

---

## Ne0n Legion
> *"Information is a commodity. We are the market."*

**Public:** Thieves and mercenaries who treat every run as a profit opportunity. They move like
couriers and think like opportunists.

**Strengths:** High Speed, strong Stealth, opportunistic Tech. Excels on smash-and-grab runs through neon districts.

---

## D4rk $pider
> *"Data for blackmail. Blackmail for survival."*

**Public:** Dark-web operators using courier lanes for blackmail, scams, and survival.

**Internal:** Their exact backers remain deliberately unclear.

**Strengths:** Maximum Tech, strong Stealth. The Grid is their natural habitat.

---

## The Asclepians
> *"Medicine moves. People live. No questions asked."*

**Public:** Medical couriers and humanitarian smugglers moving medicine, organs, and restricted care
through hostile territory.

**Internal:** They can hide high-risk medical cargo behind clean courier profiles when the route needs plausible cover.

**Strengths:** Good Speed, high Grit, strong community access. Thrives in Batteryville and Nightshade.

---

## The Mesopotamian Society
> *"Indiana Jones on an electric mountain skateboard."*

**Public:** Artifact couriers and academic treasure hunters moving relics for museums, collectors,
and dangerous patrons.

**Internal:** They are one of the quiet connectors between elite collecting culture and cult pressure.

**Reveal:** Their work entangles courier routes with the sacred object the Knights want.

**Strengths:** Maximum Rep, high Tech. Elite access to high-security archives. Penalty: limited Stealth.

---

## Hermes' Squirmies
> *"Any job. Any package. Any risk. Price adjusted accordingly."*

**Public:** A neutral courier union whose entire value proposition is secrecy without ideology. If
the job is real, they'll price it.

**Strengths:** Balanced stats. Neutral reputation. Works across all districts without faction penalties.

---

## UCPS
> *"Sanctioned delivery. Corporate rates. No questions."*

**Public:** The official board-riding postal workforce of Airaway. UCPS roles double as some of the
best cover identities in the setting.

**Strengths:** Good Speed, solid Rep. Moves through corp-controlled districts with reduced scrutiny.

---

## The Team
> *"Coordination wins races. Coordination wins everything."*

**Public:** A neutral collective of ex-athletes whose synchronized operations make them competitive
with the City's biggest powers.

**Internal:** Keep **The Team** in the same named-power conversation as the other major blocs even
when they present as sponsor-friendly performers rather than ideological operators.

**Strengths:** High Speed, high Grit. Exceptional in team-based operations. Strong Rep through visible performance.
